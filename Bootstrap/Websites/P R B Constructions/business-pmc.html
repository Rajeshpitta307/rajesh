P R B PMC

We have consulted with some really prestigious clients in our journey and the fact that they keep coming back to us testifies our commitment to excellence. Conceived by us or not, when we manage a project, we do it with absolute ownership and dedication.

  • We believe that management processes should be dynamic in nature and subject to regular review and innovation, so that the focus on overall project intent is never lost. A process function that is not adding value to the project goals will be reviewed and changed on a continuous basis.
  • Project Management is a part of our core service and it is a result of the understanding of our clients' needs. We identify which configuration of our services will achieve our clients' required outcome. We then provide solutions to achieve these objectives, while also adding value and completing projects within budget.
  • Our teams look beyond conventional PMC services by adding value at every step of the project and thereby enabling us to move ahead of our competition. Our value-adds are proposed as an advantage to both the developers and the future occupants of a project. The rich experience we have gained from successful projects that we have been involved with constantly helps us to overcome complex issues in a project life-cycle.

Our project is sectioned into the following components

  • Scope of Work Management
  • Planning and Design Management
  • Resource Management
  • Cost Management
  • Procurement and Quality Management
  • Environmental, Health & Safety Management
